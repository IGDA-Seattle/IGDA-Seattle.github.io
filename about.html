About IGDA Seattle

IGDA Seattle is a local chapter of the International Game Developers Association, a 501(c)(6) non-profit professional association and a global network comprised of individuals from all fields of game development - from programmers and producers to writers, artists, QA and localization.

IGDA Seattle brings together developers and empowers them to positively affect their career and the industry with:

  • Speaker events and the opportunity to learn from each other
  • Networking and meaningful connections with fellow game makers
  • Online resources, videos, contacts, and discussions

IGDA Seattle relies entirely on the volunteer work provided by our members, and the generosity of local game studios & venues.
